About Me

Hannah Taylor

Hi! My name is Hannah Taylor. All throughout my life I have been infatuated with sewing and creating beautiful works with fabric. My journey as a seamstress started early when I received my first sewing kit at the age of 8. Since then I started sewing everything from pillows to a very lopsided pair of shorts. I have made mistakes and have learned so much since then. I have honed onto a style and have fallen in love with dress making. I hope to share my work and improve my skills. This website will be a portfolio of my work as well as my progress.
